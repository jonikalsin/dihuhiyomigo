Andrew Siciliano NFL Wikipedia

Siciliano began his journalism career at WMAQ-670 AM  in Chicago as a reporter and anchor where he hosted the  Chicago Bears’ post-game talk show. He won  Best Anchor and Best Sports Reporter at the AIR Awards.

Andrew joined Fox Sports Radio in 2000 where he worked as a fill-in anchor and the co-host of The Tony Bruno Morning Extravaganza show. He was transferred to hosting the weeknight program Game Time Live, with Krystal Fernande. He was also the lead play-by-play announcer for the Las Vegas Gladiators of the Arena Football League.

Andrew was also the co-host of LA Sports Live alongside Mychal Thompson at 710 AM (KSPN) until January 2011. He was also a  play-by-play announcer, alongside Torry Holt and Marshall Faulk as color commentators for the broadcasts for the St. Louis Rams preseason games.

Since 2005, Andrew has been working for NFL Network as a play-by-play voice announcer for Los Angeles Rams. and hosts NFL Sunday Ticket Red Zone and NFL Total Access.
