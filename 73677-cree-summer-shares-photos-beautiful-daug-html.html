Cree Summer shares photos of her beautiful daughters who proudly show off their sister bond

- AmoMama has just learned from 'Wikipedia' and 'Instagram' where Cree shared a picture of her beautiful daughter who proudly showed off their sister bond.

- The actress was obviously overjoyed as she revealed the big news on her Instagram page while sharing a picture of the duo in the studio.

- Cree Summer has two doting daughters named Brave Littlewing and Hero Peregrine.

Cree Summer is a forty-eight-year-old actress and voice actress.

The actress is famous for her role as Winifred "Freddie" Brooks on the NBC sitcom "A Different World" through which she gained global fame and plaudits.

The forty-eight-year-old actress is also a talented voice actress as she has been the voice of different iconic cartoon characters.

She voiced Numbuh 5 in 'Codename: Kids Next Door', 'Magma in X-Men Legends', and Susie Carmichael in the iconic cartoon 'Rugrats' to name but a few.

It certainly seems like her talent for voice acting has been passed down to her two daughters as evidenced by recent news.

Cree's two daughters named Brave Littlewing and Hero Peregrine bagged their first voice-over roles for Disney last year.

Her kids voiced characters in 'Vampirina' which was a new show set to air on the DisneyJr channel in recent past months.

The doting mother was obviously proud and full of joy as she recorded close-up videos of her bundles of joys during their time in the studio.

Cree's Instagram page is filled with different loving pictures of her two bundles of joy Brave and Hero.

She captioned some of them:

'THE SAVAGES'.
