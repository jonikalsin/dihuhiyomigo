Tracey McShane: What does Jon Stewarts wife do for a living?

Aver popular American former veterinary technician, graphic designer, and animal rights activist is Tracey Lynn Stewart, née McShane.

She is well-known for being the spouse of Jon Stewart, a comedian, novelist, political commentator, and television personality.

Tracey, a Pennsylvania native, studied at Drexel University and California College of Arts & Crafts before beginning her career as a graphic designer. She made the decision to return to school to earn a degree in veterinary science after learning that her husband had been hired to anchor “The Daily Show.”

In addition to founding “Moomah the Magazine,” she also holds the position of vice president for the Portrait Project. Tracey has a close relationship with the animal welfare group Farm Sanctuary, and she and her husband just bought a farm in New Jersey to use as a home for rescued farm animals.
