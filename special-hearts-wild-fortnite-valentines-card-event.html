Special Hearts Wild Fortnite Valentines card event

Fortnite is a very popular battle royale game game distributed by Epic Games. Likewise the game has garnered massive popularity throughout the world since its release. Moreover the game has a lasting fan base, dissolving the rumours of the game dying. The game runs various events and one such is running now. Therefore in this article we take a look at the Wild Fortnite Valentine’s card event and how players can use it.

The developers of Fortnite are notorious for frequent crossovers and collaborations. Likewise the game has collaborated with many special movies, series and other games. Valentines Day is right around the corner and the developers have released several events to celebrate.

Some of these events have already started in the game as small quests. Players can complete these to get special in-game cosmetics and such. Likewise these will be based on the Valentine’s theme. Fortnite Valentine’s card event

The latest small event by Fortnite is the Special Hearts Wild Fortnite Valentine’s card event. Players can send their loved ones, free Fortnite themed Valentine’s cards.

Fishstick, Lovely and the gang wants to send the players the Valentine’s season greetings. Therefore developers have created free, printable Hearts Wild themed templates. Players can download them and edit and share to their duo partner or best friend.
