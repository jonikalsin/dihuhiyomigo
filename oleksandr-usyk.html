Biography

Oleksandr Usyk was born in Russia on January 17, 1987. He is the 2008 lightweight European Amateur Boxing Champion, 2011 heavyweight World Amateur Boxing Champion, and 2012 heavyweight gold medalist at the 2012 London Olympic Games. He took home gold at the 2012 London Games just as Luke Campbell did in the batamweight division. He achieved an Olympic gold medal before turning professional which he did in late 2013 in a fight against Felipe Romero that he won by TKO.
