All Chronolith Trial Locations in Final Fantasy 16 (FF16)

Here are all the Chronolith Trial Locations in Final Fantasy 16.

Final Fantasy 16 is the latest from the famous Final Fantasy series. The game is well known for its combat since the very first installment. Final Fantasy 16 adds more excitement to the combat mechanics of the game. There are a lot of different types of combat challenges in the game, which makes the game more unique.

Chronoliths are mysterious tablets that the players can find around different locations in Valisthea. Each Chronolith will give the players a combat task focused on a specific Eikon. There are a total of seven Chronoliths available in Valisthea. In this guide, we will provide you with all the Chronolith Trial Locations in Final Fantasy 16.

All Chronolith Trial Locations

Phoenix: The Hand of Hyperion

Phoenix’s Chronolith Trial, The Hand of Hyperion can be found at the Grand Duchy of Rosaria. It is located on the northeastern side of Martha’s Rest and west of Rhiannon’s Ride. Finishing this trial will reward the players with The Favor of Fire (Flames of Rebirth).

Garuda: The Hand of Dione

Garuda’s Chronolith Trial, The Hand of Dione can be found at the Dhalmekian Republic. It is located on the northeastern side of The Dalimil Inn and west of Drakos Deep. Finishing this trial will reward the players with The Will of Wind (Aerial Blast).

Ramuh: The Hand of Lapetus

Ramuh’s Chronolith Trial, The Hand of Lapetus can be found at the Holy Empire of Sanbreque. It is located on the northern side of the Caer Norvent River Gate and to the east of Norvent Valley. Finishing this trial will reward the players with The Favor of Lightning (Judgment Bolt).

Titan: The Hand of Titan

Titan’s Chronolith Trial, The Hand of Titan can be found at the Dhalmekian Republic. It is located on the northeastern side of the Velkroy Desert. Finishing this trial will reward the players with The Favor of Earth (Earthen Fury).

Bahamut: The Hand of Mimas

Bahamut’s Chronolith Trial, The Hand of Mimas can be found at the Holy Empire of Sanbreque. It is located on the northeastern side of Northreach and west of Cape Orsiere. Finishing this trial will reward the players with The Favor of Light (Gigaflare).

Shiva: The Hand of Rhea

Shiva’s Chronolith Trial, The Hand of Rhea can be found in the Kingdom of Waloed. It is located on the southwestern side of Rikmal’s Roost and to the west of Eistla. Finishing this trial will reward the players with The Will of Ice (Diamond Dust).

Odin: The Hand of Enceladus

Odin’s Chronolith Trial, The Hand of Enceladus can be found in the Kingdom of Waloed. It is located on the southern side of Vidargraes. Finishing this trial will reward the players with The Will of Darkness (Dancing Steel).
