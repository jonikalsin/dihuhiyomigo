Hannah Berner is not your typical reality TV star. She’s a comedian, a podcast host, a mental health advocate and a former tennis player who lives life on her own terms. She made a splash on Bravo’s Summer House, where she cracked jokes, stirred drama and won fans with her fearless and honest attitude. She’s more than just a summer house star – she’s a year-round comedy queen who makes people laugh and feel less alone.

Wiki/Bio Facts

Early Life

Hannah Berner was born on the 12th of August, 1991, in Brooklyn, New York. Born in New York, Hannah spent much of her early life in Shelter Island. Little is known about Hannah’s parents and siblings, and the reality star is not to keen on disclosing personal details. While Hannah never talks about her parents, she did say that her motto “one should not ask for a lighter burden but the broader shoulders”, was something she received from her father.
After graduating from the Poly Prep Country Day School and The Beacon School, Hannah enrolled at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. Hannah graduated with a Bachelor of Arts degree with a major in Communication Arts and Rhetoric and Certificate in Gender and Women’s Studies. She was into sports from a young age and played tennis at the national level throughout University.

Professional Career

The Brooklyn native started her career as a tennis player, playing competitively at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. She then switched gears and pursued her passion for comedy and media, working as a video producer for Betches.

Hannah joined the cast of Bravo’s Summer House in 2019, where she showcased her witty personality, hilarious commentary and romantic drama. She quickly became a fan favorite and a key member of the show. She also formed a close friendship with fellow cast member Paige DeSorbo, with whom she launched the podcast Giggly Squad in 2020. The podcast features the duo’s candid conversations about pop culture, dating, mental health and more.

Hannah also hosts her own podcast, Berning In Hell, where she interviews celebrities, influencers and comedians about their personal demons and insecurities.

In addition to her podcasts and comedy gigs, Hannah is also a co-host of Bravo’s Chat Room, a late-night talk show that features four Bravo personalities discussing the latest gossip and trends. Hannah joined the show in 2020 along with Gizelle Bryant, Kate Chastain and Porsha Williams.

Hannah is also an advocate for mental health and uses her platform to raise awareness and destigmatize mental health issues. She often shares her own struggles and experiences with anxiety, depression and therapy on her social media accounts. And, She also posts funny videos, memes, selfies and sponsored content for various brands. 

Net Worth

Hannah Berner has a net worth of around $0.5 million. She earns this fortune from her appearances on Summer House and Bravo’s Chat Room, from her podcasts Berning In Hell and Giggly Squad, as well as from her social media sponsorships.

Stand-up on the side

While Hannah primarily works as a podcast host and spent a brief time in reality TV, she loves stand-up comedy and wants to work as a full-time comedian. Hannah came to the comedy scene by directing, editing, and acting in videos on Instagram and writing viral tweets. Since then, Hannah has been performing in NYC clubs while trying to land her very own Netflix Special. Talking about her love for stand-up Hannah revealed,

“I did 10 minutes of stand-up and I really fell in love with it. Then I got up to 20 minutes, and now I’m at around 50 minutes,” she says. “The reality show isn’t my creative outlet, and I need a creative outlet. Stand-up has been that for me. I realized that I love being in front of an audience and I also love to write.”

Personal Life

She first dated Luke Gulbranson, a model and hockey coach, who she met on the show in 2019. They had a steamy hookup in April 2020 during season 4 of the show, but their relationship was full of drama and miscommunication, as Hannah claimed they had dated for a few months, while Luke insisted it was only a fling.

She then found her soulmate in Des Bishop, an Irish-American comedian. They had an instant connection and started dating in July 2020. After eight months, they got engaged in March 2021. Finally, They tied the knot on a beach in the Hamptons on May 13, 2022, surrounded by their close friends and family.
