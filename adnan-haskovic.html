Biography

Adnan Haskovic was born in Bosnia And Herzegovina on December 23, 1984. Known for his roles in such Bosnian films as Body Complete and Orange Blanket, Haskovic also acted in the Korean science fiction movie Snowpiercer and played recurring roles on Pecat, Kucni Ljubimci, and other television series. He appeared with Penelope Cruz in the 2013 movie Twice Born. After graduating from Sarajevo’s Academy of Performing Arts, he began his stage acting career as part of the ensemble of the Sarajevo War Theater.
