Early Life and Childhood

Adam Montoya came to this world on June 12, 1984, in San Diego, California, USA. Currently, he is 39 years old and his zodiac sign is Gemini. He has a sister but her name is not revealed. Moreover, he studied television, film, and new media production at San Diego State.

He worked for Machinima Games before beginning a full-time YouTube career in 2011. Apart from creating and editing YouTube videos, Adam is also passionate about skateboarding and other sports. Moreover, he owns two cats.

In detail

After graduating from San Diego State, instead of finding stable employment, Montoya did freelance work such as filming weddings. At that time, Adam, finding inspiration from other early YouTube gaming commentators, decided to record and upload his game commentaries.

Adam created a channel named ‘SeaNanners Gaming Channel’ on March 3, 2009. After the channel gained initial hype for his ‘Call of Duty’ commentaries, he eventually switched gears to PC games and ‘Minecraft’ which now stands as the number one reason for his success. However, with tons of gaming video uploads that fetched him enough fame to make his channel popular among gaming buffs, Adam quit Machinima.com to focus on his newfound career.

Work with Machinima and JETPAK

Montoya uploaded a video on December 3, 2009, to his channel explaining that he was now partnered with Machinima Inc. And that they would post gameplay videos of his to their network channel. Similarly, on June 14, 2010, Montoya posted a video to his channel stating that he was a full-time employee of Machinima.

Montoya announced that he and his associates had launched an MCN, JETPAK, on November 5, 2014. Therefore, he wanted to create a network that did not contractually take advantage of YouTubers and their channels. The MCN is operated by former Machinima employees.

The Paranormal Action Squad

On November 2, 2016, Montoya announced he would co-star with Scott Robison and Evan Fong in a new YouTube Red original series, The Paranormal Action Squad. The animated comedy aired on November 16, 2016. Therefore, the show is an animated adult comedy starring Eddie, Paul, and Vanoss with Michael Rowe, of Futurama fame, behind the show’s writing. This is YouTube Red’s 12th original series.

Break from content creation

In 2018, he slowed his YouTube video release rate from at least one video per week for most of his solo career to only releasing five videos in 2018. He also made only one tweet in 2018, a huge departure from his previous steady rate of at least one tweet per week. Adam has not officially stated anything about a permanent retirement, but in guest appearances on friends’ videos, he has discussed taking a break from YouTube and video creation to get “passive income up and running, whether I’m doing this or doing that […] looking heavily into real estate”.
